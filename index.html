Meeting Summary


Every meeting, we start by singing the National Anthem of the United States, which can be heard here:

Then, we take attendance to make sure that everyone is present, unless excused. After that, we like to start off the actual meeting by discussing a recap of the previous meeting. Next, we go over our agenda and topics for the current meeting. We discuss each topic in order of priority. After discussing all of the necessary topics, we open the meeting up for questions that anyone may have. Once all questions are taken care of, we end the meeting and everyone is excused. After finally ending the meeting, we usually like to relax and play some video games to de-stress after the long day.
